About

res is the commercial music alias of REMMI (Rachel Smith), the LA-based vocalist, songwriter, and composer whose voice and vibe has powered global campaigns for Apple, Crayola, TikTok and Aldi Germany. From original scoring to vocal-driven bangers, res specializes in music that reaches forward and feels distinctively fresh. Projects are developed independently or in collaboration with a trusted roster of producers, artists, and mixers—selected to match each client's creative direction and sonic goals.

Offerings

  • custom scoring, including vocals, for branded content.
  • vocal features, top-lining & voiceovers.
  • library pulls and light remixing for tight deadlines.
  • collaborative production with a personal network of producers, artists and engineers.
