From original scoring to vocal-driven bangers, <i>res</i> specializes in music that reaches forward and feels distinctively fresh. Projects are developed independently or in collaboration with a trusted roster of producers, artists, and mixers—selected to match each client’s creative direction and sonic goals.<br>
